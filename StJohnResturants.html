The Banana Deck Aloha and welcome to The Banana Deck, home of St John's best hand-cut Ribeye's. Our specialty is great food and service with an atmosphere to match. Sit in our open air dining room or our newly remodeled bar and enjoy the incredible views of the crystal clear water of Cruz Bay. Caribbean Bar Food, Happy Hour The Banana Deck Website
Extra Virgin Bistro At Extra Virgin Bistro we are dedicated to locally grown and sourced products, so much so that we grow most of our own herbs on site. We buy most of our fish from local fishermen and you will often find their names printed on our menu next to the fish they brought us that day. Farm to table, signiture cocktails, more fancy than other restaurants but still casual Extra Virgin Bistro Website
The Longboard Our main menu features poke bowls, ceviches, gourmet tacos & baja bowls, as well as vegetarian options, all prepared with a Caribbean flair using local spices, locally-caught seafood and locally-grown herbs, greens and fruits. The bar menu offers a unique selection of house-crafted cocktails, beers, wines & specialty drinks, including our world-famous Frozen Painkiller. Mexican Cusine, casual, great cocktails The Longboard Website
